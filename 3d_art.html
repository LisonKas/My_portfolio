VELOCITIMAC

This here is a school project where we had to model and animate a vehicle of our choice. After that, our professor did a montage of all our projects.
I chose to do a low poly airship, with a baby dragon hanging on its bow. The airship is swinging on itself due to the wind. Therefore, the dragon must balance with its wing, his tail is swinging as it is happy to play in the sky.
I chose the low poly rendering to have a cartoonish effect, with a texturing and an HDRI for the light. Enjoy the video !
